# Summoner Contracts

All DAOs on the DAOhaus platform are launched with factory contracts. We have developed a base summoner factory. This contract is indexed by our subgraphs and must be used for the [Admin](https://admin.daohaus.club/) app deployed to `admin.daohaus.club` to work with the DAO.

There are also Higher Order Summoner factories. These enable custom DAO configurations and call our base factory so they are indexed and available in our apps.
