# Summoner

Get ready to embark on an exciting journey in the world of Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs)! With the DAOhaus Summoner App, creating a brand new Moloch v3 DAO is at your fingertips.

If you've ever dreamed of conjuring up your very own DAO, the Summoner App is your magic wand. It's a powerful, yet user-friendly tool that streamlines the DAO creation process, helping you transform your vision into a reality.

Starting your journey is as simple as a click. From the DAOhaus home page, select the `Summon a DAO` button to launch the Summoner App. Here, you'll be guided through a few easy steps to configure and launch your DAO. You can define membership rules, voting protocols, and more, shaping your DAO to fit your unique vision and goals.

Whether you're new to DAOs or a seasoned wizard, the Summoner App is designed with you in mind. It's an intuitive, step-by-step interface for deploying a Moloch v3 DAO contact. So, why wait? Step into the world of DAOhaus and start shaping the future of decentralized collaboration today!
