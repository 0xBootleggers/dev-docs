# DAO

The DAO page offers a an overview of the organization and tokens.

Here you'll find the following information:

## Overview

- **Icon:** The graphic identity of the DAO.
- **Name:** The name of the DAO.
- **Contract Address:** The contract address associated with the DAO.
- **Total in Safes:** The total amount of funds held securely in the DAO.
- **Members:** The number of members participating in the DAO.
- **Proposals:** The number of proposals that have been submitted in the DAO.
- **Active Proposals:** The count of proposals currently under consideration in the DAO.

![dao-overview](https://github.com/0xBootleggers/user-guide/assets/522182/30179760-457e-4cfd-abaf-9d882990baac)

## Tokens

- **Voting:** The name and total supply of the DAO's voting tokens.
- **Non-Voting:** The name and total supply of the DAO's non-voting tokens.
