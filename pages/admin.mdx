# Overview

[Admin App](https://admin.daohaus.club/) is your central hub for operating and engaging with your DAOs.

Here's a brief overview of the key sections:

- [Hub](admin/hub) is your entry point for all the DAOs to which you belong. You can see a broad view of all your organizations from this location.
- [DAO](admin/dao) provides information about a specific organization you select. Use it to get an overview and insights about individual DAOs.
- [Proposals](admin/proposals) are a comprehensive list of all proposals submitted by a specific DAO. This section keeps you updated on the latest proposals and provides a history of decisions made in your orgnization.
- [Safes](admin/safes) is a your entry point to the main DAO treasury and any additional Gnosis Safes that have been added to your organization.
- [Members](admin/members) presents a list of all members in this organization. It's your go-to directory to view all the members contributing to a DAO.
- [Settings](admin/settings) provides access to metadata, contracts, and configuration settings for the organization. This is where you tweak the settings to align with the DAO's requirements.
- [Profile](admin/profile) is your Member Profile in this organization. Use it as an overview of your personal details within the DAO.
